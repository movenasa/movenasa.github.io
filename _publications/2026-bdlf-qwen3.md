BDLF-Qwen3 dynamically fuses multi-layer Qwen3-Embedding representations for cross-architecture binary similarity detection. Powered by a two-stage training strategy and the novel Cross-Bin dataset, it significantly outperforms existing methods at scale and demonstrates robust zero-shot generalization on unseen RISC-V architectures.
